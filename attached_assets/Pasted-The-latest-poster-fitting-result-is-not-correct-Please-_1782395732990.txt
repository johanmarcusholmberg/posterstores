A 50x70 poster has aspect ratio 5:7 = 0.714.
A 3:4 card area has aspect ratio 0.75.

* slight cropping,
* slight distortion,
* or tiny side/background gaps.

Choose no cropping. Never distort the image. Tiny background gaps are acceptable if needed.
